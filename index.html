How I learned to walk

A tale as old as time

Refusal to walk

Once i was a young boy who could not walk. My mother told me that to live I must walk. I refused this idea from a young age and continued my life as a non walker (as they would often call me).

Step by step

As I became a teenager that continued to move along the ground as others around me walked and even ran, I began to feel a distance from my peers. It was not the fact that i could not walk but more so the angle in which people looked down upon me.

The power of angles

The first step
